Shaker Heights & Me

Shaker Heights sits on the eastern edge of Cleveland, Ohio. I lived in Shaker, as it's known, from age 2 to 13. So while I wasn't born there, and I didn't go to high school there, I did enough growing up in Shaker for me to consider it my hometown. Primary vestiges from my time in Shaker Heights include progressive values and borderline obsession with the Cleveland Indians.
